"To create a new account in the Sentinel Data Vault \n(SDV) from the login screen:
Click on the \"Create new Account Button\",
When the account creation screen appears, fill in all the fields that are displayed,\n
Keep in mind that passwords MUST include at least one of ALL of the following:\n
Uppercase letter, lowercase letter, special character, and a Number.\n
The username is the email address you wish to associate with the account (eg: sentinel@datavault.org).\n
To create your account, click the \"Create my account\" button. Any fields that contain errors or remain\n
empty will keep the account from being created until such errors are corrected.\n"
